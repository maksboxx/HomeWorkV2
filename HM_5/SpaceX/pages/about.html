About Us

Our Mission

At SpaceX, our mission is to make space travel accessible and affordable for everyone. Founded in 2002 by Elon Musk, we are committed to revolutionizing space technology with the ultimate goal of enabling people to live on other planets.

Who We Are

Space Exploration Technologies Corp., or SpaceX, is a private American aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company headquartered in Hawthorne, California. We are pioneers in designing, manufacturing, and launching some of the world's most advanced rockets and spacecraft.

Our Achievements

  • First Privately-Funded Rocket to Reach Orbit: In 2008, our Falcon 1 rocket made history.
  • First Private Company to Reach the International Space Station (ISS): In 2012, our Dragon spacecraft delivered cargo to the ISS.
  • Revolutionary Reusable Rockets: Our Falcon 9 rocket's first stage has been successfully landed and reused multiple times, significantly reducing the cost of space access.
  • Manned Space Flights: In 2020, we became the first private company to send astronauts to the ISS aboard our Crew Dragon spacecraft.

Our Vision

SpaceX envisions a future where humanity is a multiplanetary species. By making space travel affordable and sustainable, we aim to create opportunities for exploration, scientific discovery, and the expansion of human civilization into space.
